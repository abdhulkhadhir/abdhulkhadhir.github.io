About Me

Passionate about transforming transportation systems

I am a passionate traffic and transportation engineer with a PhD, eager to tackle complex challenges across all modes of road transport. With hands-on experience in real-time traffic operations and expertise in tools like Aimsun, SIDRA, and VISSIM, I focus on optimizing road capacity, reducing congestion, and developing smarter solutions to improve safety and efficiency for both private vehicles and public transport.
